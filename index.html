About Me?

Hey, I'm a Software Engineer by profession


and a writer/designer by heart.

Intro

A noobie in the tech industry, gaining experience by playing around. I've tried my hands as a developer in full-stack web development. Though I like both backend and front-end engineering, but later fascinates me more. I have a deep interest in designing that I use to enhance my UI/UX skills.







Background

Born and raised in Gwalior, MP, India. Growing up, I had a unique perspective of the world - having both a spiritual and a modernistic mindset. I have lived in Gwalior for almost two decades now, and I know the city by its roots, profound culture, people, and native growth.

I did my schooling at St. Paul's School. Currently, pursuing my college education at Atal Bihari Vajpayee Indian Institute of Information Technology & Management (IIIT) Gwalior.







Interests

I found a love for poetry in my early years of college life, and sometimes like to write poetic pieces. Eventually, that helped me to realize my potential as a content writer. So as to utilize my writing skills, I took up a few content internships at known firms. Now, I spend my time coding and writing tech/non-tech blogs.

Apart from that, I'm a major art fan and enjoy exploring colors through oil pastel crayons. I feel art is the best way to connect with the all-mighty supreme power. I have several paintings associated with Gautam Buddha, music, portraits (sketches), etc.







Perspective

I believe “Life has a beautiful way of making sense eventually.” By that, I mean life is a collection of journeys, and each journey has something vast associated with it that ultimately connects when look back.
